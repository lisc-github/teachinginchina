Chinese Law and Regulations

A Chinese visa is a permit issued by the Chinese visa authorities to an alien for entry into,exit from or transit through China.The Chinese visa authorities may issue a Diplomatic,Courtesy,Official or Regular Visa to an alien according to his/her status,purpose of visit to China or passport type.

Visa Applications

About Us

US-CHINA Education and Culture Exchange Center (UCE), is a non-political, non-religious, and non-profit organization committed to develop a needed symbiosis between China and USA separated by 10,000 miles. Our mission is to foster cross-cultural understanding between Chinese and Americans. The organization achieves this goal by providing hundreds of Americans teaching opportunities to work and live in China each year.

 

As one of the first established organizations in America, we have extensive experience in arrange foreign teachers to schools and colleges throughout China. We really understand the needs and concerns of teachers coming to a foreign county to work and live. For the participants who have the sincere desire to come to China to teach while at the same time to learn about the richness and uniqueness of Chinese culture, UCE will provide comprehensive, professional services to ensure all participants work and live in China comfortable.
