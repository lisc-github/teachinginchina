Program - Teach Science

With the internationalization of education,some Chinese schools not only require foreign teachers who can teach English, but also require foreign teachers who can teach other causes of science, such as physics, math, computer, chemistry etc.

Most of these schools are foreign language schools and international schools instruct the children of expatriates living in China. These schools cater to diplomats, employees of corporations, and families living in China to provide a different environment for their children, so as to enable them keep up with their peers in their own country.

Most schools require foreign teachers to teach oral English, but depending on the particular school you are placed in, they may be asked to teach causes of Science.

Teaching at colleges, you may be asked to teach Science of the following courses: Information and Computing Science, Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, Bionomics, Statistics, Marine Sciences etc.

Some international bilingual School and foreign language middle schools need foreign teachers to teach cause of Science. The causes may be some A-Level Causes, or Act Causes such as Accounting, Computer, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Environmental science etc.

Most elementary schools require foreign teachers to teach English as while as some causes of Science. The causes may be Math, Nature science.

Program time

One Year Program

One year program of teaching in China is a unique opportunity for the people who want to spend an extended period of time in China. Working in schools, being immersed in Chinese culture is a good way to explore and experience China that a normal tourist cannot.

detail

Two Semesters Program

Two semesters program of teaching in China is also designed for those people who want to live and work in China for long time so as to experience Chinese culture and China's rapid development with each passing day.

detail

Half Year Program

Schools of China have two semesters in one academic year.Half year program of teaching in China includes teaching work of one semester and some winter camps in winter holiday.If it's the first time for you to come to China,you are not sure if you will adapt to the local life,you can choose this program

detail

One Semester Program

One semester program of teaching in China designed for the people who can teach in China for one semester. Teaching time of one semester in China will be too fast, you will expect for the next year.

detail

Weeks Program

Weeks program of teaching in China is aimed at the people who want to gain some extra teaching experience while traveling.Although these are intense work environments,it is well worth it.

detail

Flexible Program

The best time to start a teaching work in China is in September, when the schools return, or in February, just after the winter holiday or spring festival. But if you cannot catch the best time to come to China, you can choose this flexible program.
