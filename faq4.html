About teaching program

What documents should I submit when I applies for a teaching program ?

Please provide us with the following:

1). Your resume/CV via email.

2). Copy of the first page of your passport with such information as your passport number, expiration date and date of birth.

3). Copy of your highest degree and any teaching certificates you might have.

4). When you will be available to start teaching and the expected duration of your stay.

5). Your preferred location and desired age group and type of school.
