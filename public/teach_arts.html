Program - Teach Arts

Most schools require foreign teachers to teach oral English,but depending on the particular school you are placed in,they may be asked to teach arts.

Teaching at colleges,you may be asked to teach arts of the following courses: English & American literature,linguistics,English reading,English composition, English grammar,Cultural survey of English-speaking countries,International relations,tourism management Music,World history and English for Special Purposes such as business English,English for Mechanics,English for Mechanics,International Trade English,Western Accounting English,English for Marketing,English for Economics etc.

Some international bilingual School and foreign language middle schools need foreign teachers to teach cause of arts. The causes may be some A-Level Causes, such as Economics, Business Studies, Languages, History, Geography, Music, Art and Design,Psychology, Law etc. The causes may be some ACT or GAT causes, such as Information Technology, Business, management, social science etc.

Most elementary schools require foreign teachers to teach English, but in some cases, they require foreign teachers to teach some causes of arts. The causes may be Music, Fine arts, Gymnastics, Singing or Dancing.

Program time

One Year Program

One year program of teaching in China is a unique opportunity for the people who want to spend an extended period of time in China. Working in schools, being immersed in Chinese culture is a good way to explore and experience China that a normal tourist cannot.

detail

Two Semesters Program

Two semesters program of teaching in China is also designed for those people who want to live and work in China for long time so as to experience Chinese culture and China's rapid development with each passing day.

detail

Half Year Program

Schools of China have two semesters in one academic year.Half year program of teaching in China includes teaching work of one semester and some winter camps in winter holiday.If it's the first time for you to come to China,you are not sure if you will adapt to the local life,you can choose this program

detail

One Semester Program

One semester program of teaching in China designed for the people who can teach in China for one semester. Teaching time of one semester in China will be too fast, you will expect for the next year.

detail

Weeks Program

Weeks program of teaching in China is aimed at the people who want to gain some extra teaching experience while traveling.Although these are intense work environments,it is well worth it.

detail

Flexible Program

The best time to start a teaching work in China is in September, when the schools return, or in February, just after the winter holiday or spring festival. But if you cannot catch the best time to come to China, you can choose this flexible program.
