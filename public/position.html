1. Beijing Normal University

Beijing Normal University (BNU) is located in Beijing with 738779 square meters. BNU has a history of more than 100 years, which is almost as long as the history of Chinese modern education. The University has developed from the Faculty of Education, Capital Metropolitan University established in 1902 under the concept of Establish school, prioritize teacher education, which initiated teacher training in Chinese higher education. After several times of merging and reforming since 1949, especially in the 1980s, Beijing Normal University has moved into the new age of rapid development. The University's history is an epitome of the development of modern teacher training and Chinese higher education.

BNU comprises 19 full-time schools plus 6 departments ,52 bacherlor's degree programs ,69 disciplines or sub-disciplines authorized to confer Ph.D. degrees ,127 master degree programs ,7 key research centers of ministry of education P.R.C ,2 National Basic Science Reseach and Training Institutes and 16 post-doctoral research stations.

Now ,BNU enrolls over 16000 full-time students including 8000 undergraduates ,over 7000 master's degrees candidates and doctoral candidates ,and over 10000 in Continuing Education and Oline Education .Also ,there are over 2000 students from overseas.

BNU owns a high-level research faculty of over 3000 full-time teachers Researchers, including 1100 professors and associate professors,14 academicians of the Chinese academy of science and the Chinese academy of engineering .

Huludao is a seaside city of around 600,000 people, situated in the southwest of Liaoning province. The city is well-known for its beautiful beaches, which attract many visitors in the summer. The Huludao area is also rich in history, including the ancient town of Xingcheng, which still boasts its original city walls. The Great Wall meets the sea 2 hours south of Huludao at Shanhaiguan. While Huludao is still a very traditional Chinese city, it is big enough for foreigners to live comfortably, and now has western restaurants and department stores.

Our school is the only primary school that is attached to the Huludao Education Bureau. The school specializes in its bilingual teaching program. We are the recognized advanced bilingual teaching unit in Liaoning province. We have 30 classes, more than 1500 students and 78 teachers
